# Agent Space v7.14 Boundary

状态：complete
目标：record actor policy inputs and result in queue action audit evidence

## v7.14 新增

- Record the `actor` from the queue action request or signed grant as a policy input field in `go_queue_action` audit records.
- Record `actor_policy_result` as `allow` or `deny` when the local actor policy gate is reached.
- Ensure that successful local actions and denied guest actions preserve enough audit evidence to explain the local policy decision.

## 为什么这是 v7.14

v7.13 added a local actor policy gate, but did not record the actor input or policy result in the `go_queue_action` audit evidence.

v7.14 adds the `actor` field and records `actor_policy_result` when the policy gate is reached, so policy decisions can be verified post-execution without pretending earlier grant failures ran policy.

## v7.14 不新增

- 不做 login/session identity。
- 不做 role model。
- 不做 actor policy config file。
- 不做 browser-side signing UX。
- 不开放非 localhost。
