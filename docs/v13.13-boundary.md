# v13.13 Boundary: Zone Revocation in FED_QUERY Discovery

状态：complete

目标：Use authority Zone revocation in FED_QUERY discovery so revoked workers report inactive credentials and revoked workers get no credential score boost.

## What Landed

- Node `FED_QUERY` checks signed local authority Zone revocation evidence after capability credential signature and expiry verification.
- Go `FED_QUERY` loads fixture revocation entries and validates authority Zone revocation signatures before treating a worker credential as active.
- `discovery_evidence.credential.trusted` still reports whether signed capability credentials exist, while `discovery_evidence.credential.active` fails closed to `false` when the authority Zone has revoked the worker AID or alias.
- Revoked workers get no credential score boost and no `credential_active` ranking reason; non-revoked workers keep the existing active behavior.
- `federation-gateway.test.mjs`, `go-fed-discovery.test.mjs`, and `docs-contract.test.mjs` cover the authority Zone revocation in FED_QUERY discovery boundary.

## Non-Goals

- No network revocation sync.
- No cross-Zone credential lifecycle service.
- No third-party revocation service.
- No distributed revocation registry.
- No public marketplace or token-weighted reputation.

## Why This Boundary

v13.12 made credential activity depend on verifier-owned validity windows, but a signed credential could still look active to discovery after the local authority Zone had revoked the worker. v13.13 keeps the trust decision local to the authority Zone that already signs revocation evidence.

A revoked credential can still be reported as trusted because the credential signature exists, but it is not active and does not add the discovery score boost. This keeps `trusted` and `active` separate: signed history remains inspectable, while authority Zone revocation in FED_QUERY discovery prevents revoked workers from ranking as active credential holders.

## Test Contract

The tests must fail if:

- Revoked workers report `discovery_evidence.credential.active: true`.
- Revoked workers get no credential score boost is violated by adding a credential score or `credential_active` reason.
- Non-revoked workers with valid credentials stop reporting `active: true`.
- Public docs describe the slice as network revocation sync, a distributed registry, or a third-party service instead of local authority Zone revocation evidence.
