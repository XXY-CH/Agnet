# v14.5 Boundary — Intent Decomposition

状态：complete
协议标签：`v14.5-protocol`

## 目标

v14.5 adds a narrow local-first proof surface for Swarm lifecycle step 2, `主理 Agent 拆解目标和约束`: before `FED_SWARM_OPEN`, a lead Zone can emit a signed `FED_SWARM_PLAN` frame that records how the original `intent` was decomposed into step specs.

## v14.5 新增

- Node `swarmPlan(zone, swarmId, intent, steps, policyDigest)` creates a `FED_SWARM_PLAN` frame.
- The frame carries the signing Zone descriptor and a `plan` body with `swarm_id`, original `intent`, `steps`, `policy_digest`, `plan_digest`, and `plan_signature`.
- Each step spec is local evidence for decomposition: `step_id`, `capability`, optional `constraint`, and optional `depends_on` order constraints.
- `plan_digest` is the SHA-256 digest of canonical `{ intent, steps }`.
- `plan_signature` is the Zone Ed25519 signature over the plan body.
- Node `verifySwarmPlan(frame, trustedZones)` verifies the Zone descriptor, local Zone trust, non-empty step list, required `step_id` and `capability`, NUL-free `step_id`, `policy_digest`, `plan_digest`, and `plan_signature`.
- `FED_SWARM_OPEN.swarm.plan_digest` may link execution to the decomposition proof. When supplied, the gateway copies it into `FED_SWARM_CLOSE.close.plan_digest`.
- Node `verifySwarmClose` accepts a close proof with `plan_digest` only when it is a 64-hex digest.

## v14.5 不新增

- 不做 LLM orchestration。
- 不做 automatic candidate selection。
- 不做 candidate ranking changes。
- 不做 automatic policy synthesis。
- 不做 distributed planner service。
- 不让 `verifySwarmClose` cross-reference or fetch the `FED_SWARM_PLAN`; callers verify that linkage through `plan_digest`.
- 不把 a signed decomposition plan 宣称为 full task DAG orchestration。

## 为什么这是 v14.5

v14.1 made worker commitments explicit before execution. v14.4 made failure migration visible after execution. v14.5 fills the preceding Swarm step: the lead Agent's intent decomposition becomes signed, digest-addressed evidence before `FED_SWARM_OPEN` chooses or executes workers.

## 验收

- `swarmPlan` produces a signed `FED_SWARM_PLAN` with `intent`, two step specs, `policy_digest`, `plan_digest`, and `plan_signature`.
- `verifySwarmPlan` accepts the valid plan and rejects tampered `plan_signature`.
- `verifySwarmPlan` rejects empty `steps` and NUL-bearing `step_id` values.
- A `FED_SWARM_CLOSE` carrying the linked `plan_digest` passes `verifySwarmClose`.
- A `FED_SWARM_CLOSE` with malformed non-hex `plan_digest` fails `verifySwarmClose`.
- Docs-contract tests assert the `swarmPlan`, `FED_SWARM_PLAN`, `intent`, and `plan_digest` wording without claiming LLM orchestration or automated candidate selection.
