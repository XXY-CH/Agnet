## v10.24 新增

- `scripts/public-node-proof.mjs` sends authenticated `FED_TASK_OPEN` to the public-listen gateway.
- The task targets `agent://zone-b/summarizer`.
- The proof output includes:
  - `task_id: "public_node_probe_task"`
  - `task_receipt: true`
  - `task_close: true`
- The proof watchdog is 60 seconds because full Node verification can take longer than the earlier 20 second budget after task execution is included.
