# v14.3 Boundary — Cross-zone trust chains

状态：complete
协议标签：`v14.3-protocol`

## 目标

v14.3 adds the local-first cross-zone trust chain proof surface: Zone A can hold a Zone A-signed delegation to Zone B, and FED_QUERY discovery evidence can carry that delegation as `zone_trust_chain` provenance for remote results.

## v14.3 新增

- `zoneTrustDelegation(authorityZone, delegateZoneDescriptor, capabilities)` creates a signed delegation body with `delegator`, `delegate`, `capabilities`, and `delegator_descriptor`.
- `verifyZoneTrustDelegation(delegation, trustedAuthorityDescriptor)` verifies that the delegator is the trusted local authority Zone, the delegation signature is valid, and `capabilities` is an array.
- `discovery_evidence.zone_trust_chain` is the local proof slot for cross-zone provenance. Empty `zone_trust_chain: []` means direct Zone membership with no delegation chain required.
- The implemented v14.3 verification scope is one signed Zone-to-Zone delegation record and local trusted-authority descriptor checking.

## v14.3 不新增

- 不做 DID-native universal resolver。
- 不做 network revocation sync。
- 不做 cross-zone legal liability system。
- 不把 one signed delegation record called universal trust.
- 不做 automatic P2P trust discovery。

## 为什么这是 v14.3

v14.0 opened the local-first overlay/swarm/routing line. v14.1 added signed Swarm worker commitments. v14.3 adds a narrow signed delegation primitive for local cross-zone provenance without changing the local-first trust-store model.

## 验收

- Node exposes `zoneTrustDelegation` and `verifyZoneTrustDelegation`.
- Tests prove a Zone A delegation to Zone B verifies against Zone A's trusted descriptor.
- Tests prove tampering the delegated capability or subject makes verification fail.
- Public docs and the ASP Core draft name `zone_trust_chain` and keep this as a local-first signed delegation proof surface.
