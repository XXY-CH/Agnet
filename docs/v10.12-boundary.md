# Agent Space v10.12 Boundary

状态：complete
目标：Make `FED_SWARM_CLOSE` carry a Zone-signed close proof for completed Swarm steps.

## v10.12 新增

- Go `FED_SWARM_CLOSE` now includes `close`.
- `close.swarm_id` names the closed Swarm.
- `close.step_receipts` lists completed steps in execution order with `step_id`, `task_id`, and `receipt_digest`.
- `close.close_signature` is signed by the local Zone authority key.
- `test/go-fed-discovery.test.mjs` verifies the close proof contents and signature.

## 为什么这是 v10.12

v9 made Swarm step receipts and dependencies auditable. v10.12 adds the smallest close-time proof that the Swarm lifecycle ended with a specific ordered receipt set, without adding a scheduler or dynamic decomposition.

## v10.12 不新增

- 不做 dynamic Swarm decomposition。
- 不做 scheduler-owned DAG execution。
- 不做 parallel Swarm execution。
- 不做 conflict/merge receipts。
- 不做 cross-Zone Swarm。
- 不做 Swarm UI。
- 不做 receipt store/search。
- 不做 A2A/ANP/AGNTCY compatibility。
