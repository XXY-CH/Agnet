# Code of Conduct

Agnet discussion should be technical, direct, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent, but verify claims with evidence.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Keep disagreements specific and actionable.
- Respect project boundaries and maintainer time.
- Credit prior work and external projects accurately.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private contact details or secrets.
- Repeatedly derailing scoped technical discussions.
- Misrepresenting prototype capabilities as production guarantees.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, or pull requests that violate this code. Repeated violations may lead to blocked participation.

Report serious conduct issues privately to a maintainer.
